## Description ✅
ThinkPop is the daily game habit built for adults who love a real challenge.

Four game modes. Hundreds of levels. Fresh variety every day.

**Memory Match** — Flip cards and find matching pairs before time runs out. Each level adds more pairs and less time.

**Odd One Out** — Spot the item that doesn't belong. Sounds simple. Gets seriously tricky.

**Speed Match** — Race the clock to find the target before it disappears. The faster you are, the higher your score.

**Pattern Pulse** — Watch the sequence. Remember it. Pick what comes next. Or spot what's missing.

**Your Journey**
Progress through 101 levels across 10 beautifully designed worlds — from lush forests to the cosmic finale. Each level unlocks the next. Boss levels hit every 10 stages.

**Track Your Progress**
See how you're improving across all four game types. Weekly reports show your most active days, total points earned, and your best performances.

**Built for Daily Play**
Sessions are 5–10 minutes. Perfect for a morning coffee, a lunch break, or winding down in the evening. A few levels a day keeps the fun going.

**Free to play** with 10 levels daily. Upgrade to Unlimited for endless play, full stats, and no waiting.

Download ThinkPop and start your streak today.
