## Description

ThinkPop is the brain training game that actually feels like a game.

No homework. No lectures. Just satisfying, pop-worthy challenges that keep your mind engaged — in as little as 10 minutes a day.

── HOW IT WORKS ──

Follow your journey along a winding world map. Each level is a quick brain challenge across 4 domains:

🧠 Memory Match — flip cards and find the pairs
⚡ Speed Match — tap the matching symbol before time runs out
🔍 Odd One Out — spot what doesn't belong
🌀 Pattern Pulse — predict what comes next in the sequence

Complete levels to unlock new worlds: Forest, Ocean, Desert, Mountains, Space, and beyond. Earn stars, build your streak, and watch your ThinkPop score grow.

── WHY THINKPOP ──

Most brain training apps feel like homework. ThinkPop feels like a classic pop-and-match puzzle game — but with challenges that make you think. Every correct answer, every level cleared, every new world unlocked comes with a satisfying POP of spring animation and haptic feedback.

Built for people who want to stay mentally engaged without the guilt of a "study app."

── TRACK YOUR PROGRESS ──

Your Stats dashboard tracks your performance across Memory, Speed, Logic, and Pattern challenges. A personal coach tip updates daily to keep you motivated.

── 101 LEVELS ACROSS 10 WORLDS ──

From the Forest warm-up trail to the Cosmic Finale, each world brings new challenges and a fresh visual theme. Boss levels every 10 stages test everything you've learned.

── PLAY YOUR WAY ──

No login required — start playing instantly as a guest. Sign in when you're ready to save your progress across devices.

Free to play. Optional Premium unlocks unlimited daily levels and a detailed weekly report.

ThinkPop is designed for entertainment and mental engagement.

## What to fill in before submitting

### eas.json
- `YOUR_APPLE_ID@email.com` — your Apple ID email
- `YOUR_APP_STORE_CONNECT_APP_ID` — numeric ID from App Store Connect app URL
- `YOUR_APPLE_TEAM_ID` — found at developer.apple.com under Membership

### app.json
- `YOUR_EAS_PROJECT_ID` — run `npx eas init` in terminal to get this automatically

## Build Commands

# Link project to EAS (run once)
npx eas init

# Development build (iOS Simulator)
npx eas build --platform ios --profile development

# TestFlight build
npx eas build --platform ios --profile preview

# Production build (App Store)
npx eas build --platform ios --profile production

# Submit to App Store (after production build)
npx eas submit --platform ios
